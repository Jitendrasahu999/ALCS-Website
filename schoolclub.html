Health Club

The Health Club nurtures student wellness by promoting physical, mental, and emotional health. Through interactive workshops and awareness drives, students learn the value of balanced living and personal care.

We recognize that healthy students are better learners. By focusing on nutrition, mental well-being, personal hygiene, and life skills, we aim to build a foundation for strong, confident individuals prepared to face real-world challenges.
